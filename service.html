Services

  1. Service

Building Construction

At Deck Point Construction, we specialize in creating high-quality, durable, and visually appealing buildings. Whether it’s residential or commercial, we ensure your project is delivered on time and within budget, with a focus on craftsmanship and attention to detail.

General Contracting

Our general contracting services offer comprehensive project management for a seamless construction experience. We handle every aspect of your project, from initial planning to the final touches, ensuring quality execution and efficient coordination between all parties involved.

New Home Construction

Building your dream home is our passion. From custom designs to the final build, we collaborate closely with our clients to bring their vision to life. With quality materials and expert craftsmanship, we create homes that are both beautiful and functional.

Construction Cleanup

We provide thorough post-construction cleanup services to ensure that your property is left spotless and ready for occupancy. Our team handles all aspects of cleanup, from debris removal to detailed cleaning, allowing you to focus on moving in or starting your business.

Design-Build Solutions

Our design-build solutions simplify the construction process by integrating design and construction under one roof. We work closely with clients to ensure that both the vision and execution meet expectations, delivering cost-effective and efficient results.

Plumbing & HVAC

Our plumbing and HVAC services ensure that your building has reliable systems for water, heating, and cooling. We handle everything from installations to repairs, making sure your systems run efficiently and are compliant with local regulations.
